Stuart Lazell

Stuart Lazell is an experienced commercial manager with over 30 years within the construction industry in project, cost and contract management, and in the management of all commercial aspects of both design and construction.

Stuart started in the Construction Industry in 1976 and completed a six year Bachelor of Applied Science (QS) Degree at the University of Technology Sydney. Stuart gained experience in all aspects of Quantity Surveying during a four year cadetship with WT Partnership.

Stuart then worked for several large contractors (including Woolworths Ltd (Properties), Thiess Watkins Limited, Leighton Contractors Pty Ltd and Baulderstone Hornibrook Pty Ltd) in a number of on-site commercial roles for fourteen years before then becoming an independent QS consultant in 1994.

Stuart has worked on a variety of building and engineering projects particularly residential, commercial, industrial, pharmaceutical, hospital and special purpose buildings. He has experience in Rail Infrastructure (both in Alliance and lump sum Design and Construct delivery) and with Water Infrastructure Alliancing.

As an independent QS consultant since 1994, Stuart has assisted private and institutional clients with the commercial management of developments across all sectors of the Sydney construction market. He has provided commercial advice from due diligence and feasibility stages through to full design, commercial and project management services.

Stuart was elevated to Fellow of the Australian Institute of Quantity Surveyors in 2008. He is also a certified Green Star Consultant by the Green Council of Australia.

Stuart brings to his work strong interpersonal skills, extensive experience, and a commitment to outstanding outcomes. He has a professional, disciplined, and systematic approach to managing costs and other challenges. Stuart achieves the highest quality standard of service delivery from commercial and project teams. Stuart is very much a people person and particularly enjoys working in a team environment. Stuart's focus is on successful project completion as well as being a driving team leader and member.
